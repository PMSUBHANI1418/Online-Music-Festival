Artist Lineup

DJ Snake

Known for chart-toppers like "Taki Taki" and "Let Me Love You", bringing the best of EDM and trap music.

Martin Garrix

One of the youngest superstar DJs, famous for "Animals", "Scared to Be Lonely", and unforgettable live sets.

Billie Eilish

Alternative pop sensation with a unique style and multiple Grammy awards, known for "bad guy" and "Happier Than Ever".

The Chainsmokers

EDM duo blending pop and dance, creators of hits like "Closer" and "Don't Let Me Down".

Travis Scott

Hip hop icon and festival favorite, known for his high-energy performances and hits like "SICKO MODE".
